FLOW WITHIN TRAINING

A practice of physical and mental training cultivated and taught by Dana & Kristina Heimbecker through various vectors to enhance human optimization.

We provide the tools and insight to improve mental well being, longevity and body control. Through continuous education and application to our own self practice, we have cultivated a training approach combining the following vectors:

Meditation (Mindfulness)
Joint Mobility
Body Control
Strength Development
Energy System Development (Conditioning)
Fasting & Nutrition

The Flow Within Training thought process teaches the foundations of developing a life long practice in vital areas of fitness, health and mental well-being.

Why Flow Within?

Our assimilation of the 5 vectors has been proven as an effective way to enhance your physical training and everyday life.

Programs are individualized to your lifestyle, needs and goals.

Our idealogy is a no bull-shit approach that takes hard-work, disciple & dedication.

Our Training Ethos

We believe that results are an outcome of consistent effort over a period of time with no quick fixes or shortcuts.

Our philosophy is to teach & educate you with the intent to help you develop mental well-being, physical longevity and healthy habits that are continuous & everlasting.

Our training philosophy is putting forth hard-work & effort intelligently on a continuous basis.

Dana has been involved in the study of human movement throughout his entire life. He was exposed to Martial Arts at a young age, which later lead to a NCAA Division 1 soccer scholarship at Marshall University where he obtained a B. A. in Sports Management. Following Marshall University, Dana’s soccer career continued in Europe where he pursued a professional soccer career in Belgium and Germany. Training and playing at a professional level gave him an invaluable experience that later translated to a career as a Strength and Conditioning Coach.

Dana worked as a youth athletic development coach for 5 years, training youth athletes from all different sports (swimming, tennis, soccer, basketball, hockey, rock-climbing). He also worked as an assistant Strength & Conditioning Coach for the Niagara Ice Dogs (Ontario Hockey League) from 2012 to 2018 designing and implementing training programs with professional, collegiate and junior hockey players in the Niagara Region.

Dana has trained and consulted with professional and collegiate organizations from the NBA, NFL, NHL, MLB, MLS and NCAA.

Dana is a Functional Range Conditioning Instructor and heavily involved in the Functional Range Systems curriculum created by Dr. Andreo Spina. As a FRS Master Instructor, Dana travels the world educating professional sport organizations, trainers and therapist on the Functional Range Systems (Functional Range Assessment, Functional Range Conditioning, & Kinstretch).

Dana’s involvement in this system has refined his training approach immensely as he provides a strict work ethic, fundamental and disciplined approach of training to all his students.

Dana currently works as a Rehab Coordinator and Head Trainer at Move Lab - FRS Headquarters in Toronto, Ontario where he regularly conducts team training modules and shares his expertise to the team. His database of knowledge is continuously growing in all areas of health & wellness and he continues to share his passion with his personal clients and current trainers motivated to learn more.

Kristina attended Brock University to obtain her Undergraduate Degree in Speech and Language Sciences followed by a post graduate certificate in Autism and behaviour sciences at Mohawk College. Kristina completed her 200-Hour Yoga teacher training through Yandara Yoga Institute in Baha, Mexico in 2013 which sparked the desire to continue her education from many other sources around the globe. To continue her education, she completed her Functional Range Conditioning (FRC®) Certification and Kinstretch certification shortly after. She has made incredible leaps in her movement abilities in such a short time and through practicing the system on herself, she can now transfer that experience in her teachings and guide students to reach their own mobility goals. Kristina is well known in the movements community as Functional Range Systems Social Media Manager and assists around the globe at FRC and Kinstretch certification seminars.

Kristina currently works alongside her husband Dana John Heimbecker, where they design training programs, which include strength, mobility and recovery to youth athletes and general population seeking a healthier and well-balanced movement practice.

WHAT DO WE OFFER?

COACHING

All humans strive to be better in this world, but sometimes we don’t know where to start or what to do when it comes to our physical and mental well-being.

With the many types of training methods that exist today, it is easy to become overwhelmed with what to put your time towards.

A coach helps give you:

  • Relation and insight
  • Simplicity but effectiveness in program design
  • Helps identify your goals and achieve your goals

Our coaching style will give you a simplified & effective approach
to help maximize your human potential resulting in a pain free and well balanced life.

KINSTRETCH

Kinstretch is a movement enhancement system that develops maximum body control, flexibility, and USABLE ranges of motion. Rather than being an alternative to other forms of exercise, mobility, or flexibility programs, Kinstretch is a system that prepares you for all these other things.

Having a continuous practice will improve your overall joint health so you can do other things you enjoy pain free - with your newly acquired usable ranges of motion.
